Executive Committee Meeting

The Executive Committee (ExCom) holds a meeting once a year to make key decisions on CityNet’s programs and initiatives. These meetings serve as a platform for city leaders and representatives to come together, review the progress of ongoing projects, and set the strategic direction for the Network’s future.

The ExCom consists of the President, President Emeritus, two Vice-Presidents, and nine members.
